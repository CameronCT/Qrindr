About
With the upcoming QuakeCon and Quake Pro League, players have been emulating the formats used by professional players on their streams. For many, this is still being manually done and oftentimes leads to confusion and forgetfulness.

Qrindr is designed as a userfriendly system that walks players through both map and champion selection processes. Accounts are unnecessary and player names used are simply for show.
